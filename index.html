What types of payment do you accept for equipment purchases?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, ), Bikas And Nagad, and direct bank transfers. Choose the option that suits you best during the checkout process for a seamless shopping experience.

What age groups do you cater to in your hockey training programs?

Our hockey training center welcomes participants of all ages. We offer specialized programs designed for kids, teens, and adults, ensuring that players receive age-appropriate coaching and skill development tailored to their individual needs.

What sets your private hockey lessons apart?

Our private lessons offer personalized attention from experienced coaches, tailored skill development, and immediate feedback. Elevate your game with focused, one-on-one training to achieve your hockey goals efficiently and effectively.

How can I get started with ski touring, and what equipment do I need?

To begin ski touring, consider joining introductory courses, familiarize yourself with safety protocols, and invest in essential equipment such as touring skis, boots, bindings, climbing skins, and avalanche safety gear. Our experts are available to guide you through the process and help you embark on this exhilarating outdoor adventure.

How can I book a ski touring experience with your center?

Booking your ski touring adventure is easy! Simply visit our website's booking section, choose your preferred date, select the type of tour, and complete the secure online reservation. For personalized assistance or specific inquiries, our friendly customer support team is ready to help—contact us via phone or email, and we'll ensure your booking process is seamless.

How are ski touring prices determined at your center?

Our ski touring prices are based on factors such as tour duration, group size, and equipment rentals. Visit our Pricing page on the website for a detailed breakdown of costs or contact our customer support for personalized assistance.
